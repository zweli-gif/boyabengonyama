Module 3 — Journals & Ledgers (≈3:30)
Avatar: Black presenter (friendly, calm)

On-screen: “Journal first”
Journal is the book of first entry.
It records transactions in date order.

On-screen: “Debit then credit”
A journal entry shows date, debit, credit, amount, narration.
Debits must equal credits.

On-screen: “Ledger groups by account”
Ledger groups transactions by account.
Each account has its own page.

On-screen: “Example”
Dr Trading Stock 2,500
Cr Bank 2,500

On-screen: “Recap”
Journal = date order. Ledger = account order.
