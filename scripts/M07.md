Module 7 — VAT (≈3:30)
Avatar: Black presenter (friendly, calm)

On-screen: “VAT is 15%”
VAT is a tax. Standard VAT in SA is 15%.

On-screen: “Output vs input”
Output VAT is VAT on sales.
Input VAT is VAT on purchases.

On-screen: “Payable or refundable”
VAT payable = Output − Input.

On-screen: “Quick formulas”
Excl VAT: Total = price × 1.15
Incl VAT: VAT = total × 15/115

On-screen: “Recap”
Output minus input.
