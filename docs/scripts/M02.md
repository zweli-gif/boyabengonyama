Module 2 — Double Entry & Accounting Equation (≈3:30)
Avatar: Black presenter (friendly, calm)

On-screen: “Assets = Liabilities + Capital”
Every business follows one rule:
Assets = Liabilities + Capital.

Assets are what the business owns.
Liabilities are what it owes.
Capital is the owner’s share.

On-screen: “Two accounts, always”
Double entry: every transaction affects two accounts.
One debit. One credit. They must be equal.

On-screen: “Example: Sipho’s car wash”
Sipho invests R10,000 and borrows R5,000.
Bank goes up by R15,000.
Loan goes up by R5,000.
Capital goes up by R10,000.

On-screen: “DEAD CLIC”
DEAD increases on debit.
CLIC increases on credit.

On-screen: “Recap”
Equation + double entry.
