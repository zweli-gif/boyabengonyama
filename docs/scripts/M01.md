Module 1 — Accounting Concepts & Principles (≈3:00)
Avatar: Black presenter (friendly, calm)

On-screen: “Accounting has rules”
Hello. In this lesson we learn the accounting concepts.
These concepts are rules.
They help everyone record money the same way.

On-screen: “Rule 1: Entity”
Entity concept: the business is separate from the owner.
If Zandile takes money from the till for home groceries,
that is drawings. It is not a business expense.

On-screen: “Rule 2: Going concern”
Going concern: we assume the business will continue.
So we do not treat everything as closing tomorrow.

On-screen: “Rule 3: Historical cost”
Historical cost: record assets at the price you paid.
Later you use depreciation.

On-screen: “Rule 4: Prudence”
Prudence: be careful. Do not overstate profit.
If a debtor might not pay, record a provision.

On-screen: “Rule 5: Matching”
Matching: put income and related cost in the same period.

On-screen: “Rule 6: Materiality”
Materiality: small items can be treated simply.

On-screen: “Recap”
Entity, going concern, historical cost, prudence, matching, materiality.
